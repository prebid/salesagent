🔑 Signing keys

Salesagent's outbound signing keys (RFC 9421). Buyers verify our webhook signatures against a public JWKS you publish on your own domain. Two steps:

  1. Publish the JWK below as a JWKS document ({"keys": […]}) at a URL you control — typically https://<your-house-domain>/.well-known/jwks.json. Preserve the adcp_use field on each key — the AdCP webhook verifier rejects keys without adcp_use="webhook-signing", and mixing webhook-signing keys with request-signing keys in the same JWKS only works if every key keeps its own adcp_use.
  2. Reference that URL from your brand.json's agents[].jwks_uri entry at {{ tenant.brand_json_url or 'https://<your-house-domain>/.well-known/brand.json' }}. Set jwks_uri explicitly when the salesagent runs on a different domain than your brand.json (e.g. a SaaS subdomain) — the well-known fallback only resolves when origins match.
