Trusted Match Providers are buyer-side agents that the TMP Router fans out to during ad selection. Each provider evaluates context signals and/or identity signals against their synced package set and returns scored offers. The TMP Router calls all active providers in parallel within the configured latency budget, then merges results for the publisher-side join.
